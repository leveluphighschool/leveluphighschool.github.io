Starting high school?

Level Up is your ultimate high school survival guide which will include general advice, study tips, and how to stay healthy during the next five stress-filled years of your life

~ EXPERIENCES ~

Find out what it's like to be in high school! Seven current Year 9 students have kindly shared advice and tips that allowed them to balance their academics with extra-curricular activites and their social lives. Discover what parts of high school they found interesting, challenges they faced and how they overcame them.



~ WHAT TO EXPECT ~

As with any new environment, the first few weeks of school can be quite terrifying as you test the waters and slowly get into the rhythm of school life. This page is designed to give you some insight into the kind of routine and demands that are expected from you as a Year 9 student. You'll learn how to make the most of your first day, keep track of your homework, get to your classes and so on!



~ ACADEMIC ~

It's no secret that you're expected to do so much more homework each night in high school compared to intermediate. Here, you'll find advice about the best ways you can balance your homework with YouTube time, and avoid the dreaded P word - PROCRASTINATION. Discover what methods of learning you align most strongly with and what techniques you can integrate into your study routine to get the best possible grades you want.



~ EXTRA-CURRICULAR ~

Extra-curricular activities are such a big part of life at high school. Not only do they express your commitment and involvement within the school, giving you the opportunity to receive leadership roles in your senior years (e.g. Prefect, Librarian, Leader of a Committee, Head Boy/Girl), but they also allow you to make friends with those who share your interests. Use this page to find out what activities you might be interested in participating in.
