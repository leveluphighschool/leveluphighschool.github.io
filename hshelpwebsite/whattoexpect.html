How different is high school REALLY from intermediate?
Read on to find the answer as well as how to prepare yourself. Learn the best way to know where all your classes are, how to make new friends, organisation skills and so on.
1. ENVIRONMENT
You will most likely already know that finding your way around any new environment is alway difficult. The best way to learn adjust yourself is to:
  1. Try and visit the school before classes begin

    2. Familiarising yourself by using landmarks to get a general idea of the positions of buildings can be helpful (e.g. B-block is the brick building to the right of the big tree which stands in front of the library). Even knowing just taking the note of any signposts which point to the direction of blocks of classrooms can be useful.

  2. Attend orientation day
